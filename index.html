A snapshot of movies and TV shows in Netflix

People have seldom noticed how many movies and TV shows are collected by Netflix. It seems like that most of the time, people can always find what they want to watch on Netflix. Based on a recent article, Netflix is the most-watched TV streaming service in Australia. Visualising all the Movies and TV data in Netflix might be an approach to understand its success.

Select genre

The data is obtained by accumulating the original data based on different genres. According to the quantity of movies/TV shows, top 10 genres are selected.

In the left panel, genres are displayed by alphabetical order, and you can select different genres to play the different wordcloud.

The world cloud Shows the most popular words in the titles of all movies/tv shows in Netflix and also of top 10 genre of movies/ TV Shows respectively. For all movies/tv shows, the most popular words are "love", "christmas", "story", "man" and "american". “love” is still major words can be found in different genres except for "Action & Adventure", "Children & Family Movies" and "International Movies".

This network diagram is created with the data that is aggregated by accumulating the original data based on the actor's name. To keep the simplicity, only top 10 actors who have participated in the most movies on Netflix are selected.

The network diagram reveals the connections between celebrities. In the drop-down menu below, you can select the different actors/actresses who have cacted in the most movies and TV shows on Netflix to see their network.

The red circle in the center is the cast selected while other yellow circles are the casts who have collbotated with center cast before.

Top 10 celebrities



Select one of the actors/actresses in the dropdown menu to see the relationship with other casts.

Hovering on the nodes can show the name of that cast. Scrolling mouse wheel will zoom in/out the network diagram

The data is generated from the original data by selecting ten countries with the most movie and TV show productions.

You can hover mouse on either chords, arcs or country names to highlight the specific country and see the interrelationship and proportion of that country.

The chord diagram shows United States taking the most proportion of movies/TV shows producing and it has cooperation with the remaining nine countries.
